i'm going to LIVE, dammit!

A personal project recording an album in the span of two hours with Zach Watson, then creating matching visuals and a physical release.

Each song is accompanied by an animation made by printing and rescanning frames, manipulated by way of water- and alcohol-based ink displacement, tearing, and toner cartridge failure.


Each visualizer can be seen with its respective song in the following YouTube playlist, or via Spotify Canvases.

The standard cassette J-Card was modified into a staplebound booklet containing all lyrics and guitar tabs, as well as extra information regarding the animation techniques for each visualizer, design specifications, and credits.
